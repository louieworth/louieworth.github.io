About me

Hello! I am Li Jiang (姜力), a second-year Ph.D. student at McGill and Mila - Quebec AI Institute, advised by Prof. Yichuan Ding and Prof. Xue Liu. My research interests include:

  • LLM Fine-tuning and Alignment: Reinforcement Learning with Human Feedback (RLHF) for LLMs and Scalable Oversight.
  • RL + X: Applications in Operations Management (e.g., healthcare, supply chains), with RL’s edge in prioritizing long-term value.
  • LLMs + X: Applications in Operations Management (e.g., healthcare, supply chains), leveraging LLMs’ ability to synthesize and reason over unstructured data..

I am open to collaboration, feel free to reach me out!
