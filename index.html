About me

Hello! I am Li Jiang (姜力), a third-year Ph.D. student at McGill and Mila - Quebec AI Institute, advised by Prof. Yichuan Ding and Prof. Xue Liu. My research interests include:

  • LLM Fine-tuning and Alignment: Reinforcement Learning with Human Feedback (RLHF) for LLMs and agents.
  • LLMs + X: Applications of LLMs in complex, human-centric domains, leveraging their unique strengths in reasoning over and synthesizing unstructured data.

I am open to collaboration, feel free to reach me out!
