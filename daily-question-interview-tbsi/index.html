Good morning, Professors.

My name is Li Jiang, English name is Louis, graduated from SWUST in 2018. I am 23 years old. I come from the province that is famous for its hot pot. I think Sichuan province is popping in your head now. Since Sichuan is surrounded by mountains, it really gives me a shot to develop my hobby of hiking. I really enjoy hiking. During my four-year study, my grade is top 5% in my major. In the meantime, I was granted scholarship several times such as: National Encouragement Scholarship, the Scholarship from China Scholarship Council and others in my university.

When it comes to academic aspect, I would like to describe myself with three key words.

1st Passionate. I didn’t realize that data is driving our society until I join a national competition it used the data to predict the construction waste. It generated my interests for data science and AI. You know, interest is the best teacher in one’s whole life.

2st Innovative. I am full of passion to think outside of the box. I have issued a patent and after my undergraduate study, I joined the summer program of Business and Entrepreneurship in TAU.

3st hard-working. I am not a daydreamer. I have read several books related to basic knowledge about CS and ML, but also, after the National Graduate Entrance Examination, I joined Kaggle . This competition focused on energy useage predictions in buildings. It gave me insights about how machine learning works and details about some Machine Learning models, like SVM, LightGBM and others..

TBSI is a ideal choice for me. above all of the advantages of academic aspects, TBSI and ShenZhen are very beautiful and there are a lot of mountains so that I can go hiking in my spare time.

That’s all about my introduction, thank you for your precious time and attention.

Our campus is pretty large and there are a lot of students and works in the main campus. Maybe 7,000 students. My campus has a lightspot in my campus.it include a mountain, named Dragon Mountain. When it comes to weekends or just a long period of break after the class, I would hang out together with my friends. We enjoyed the Dragon Mountain and had a lot of fun.

When it comes to my weakness, I would like to say that my biggest weakness is being too outdoorsy. you know, information is booming in this new era/‘ɪrə/, people, especially the youth generations, will spend a lot of time socializing and talking with others about different stuff on the internet. like entertainment, news and celebrities.
